Project-1: ContactMyReps
How To Use:
  • 1.- Write a valid US address.
  • 2.- Make sure to Select at least one level of Goverment to include in the search by clicking on one of the checkboxes.
  • 3.- If address is resolved Click on the Search button in order to resolve address.
  • 4.- If address successfully resolved Click on Show button in order to list representatives.
  • 5.- List will be displayed showing all the representatives assigned to that address, Click on the row of the person you are interested in contacting and a pop up with their contact info will appear.
  • 6.- After pop up is shown Click on Save to save to personal Directory, Send Email in order to contact representative if email is available, and Close to exit pop up window
